Reimbursement guide

Understanding your reimbursement

In Belgium, reimbursement of medical procedures is governed by the INAMI/RIZIV nomenclature. Depending on the nature of the intervention — medical or cosmetic — your health insurer will contribute differently.

Your surgeon is an accredited healthcare provider. At the end of the consultation, they will issue a proof-of-care certificate that you can submit directly to your health insurer with no additional steps required.

Medical procedure — Partially reimbursed

Removal of a suspicious naevus, indication confirmed by the surgeon → partial reimbursement by the health insurer based on INAMI/RIZIV nomenclature.

Cosmetic procedure — Not reimbursed

Removal for comfort or purely aesthetic reasons → full patient responsibility. Some supplementary health insurers partially cover this type of procedure — check with your insurer.

What you should bring

  • Your identity card (eID)
  • Your valid health insurance card
