Dermo-surgery

Our Dermo-Surgery Services

From dermoscopic examination to surgical removal — all in a single consultation.

Prevention

Full Skin Assessment

Our surgeon examines all your skin lesions using the Heine DeltaOne dermatoscope. Each naevus is analysed according to the ABCDE rule: Asymmetry, Border, Colour, Diameter, Evolution.

The assessment identifies lesions requiring removal, those needing regular monitoring, and those that are entirely benign. Unlike a standard dermatology consultation, our plastic surgeon can — if removal is indicated — perform it immediately during the same visit.

Indications: Annual check-up, family history of melanoma, fair skin, high sun exposure, evolving or suspicious mole.

Duration: 20–30 min  ·  Reimbursement: Standard medical consultation rate

What we analyse

  • Asymmetry of the lesion
  • Regularity of the border
  • Uniformity of colour
  • Diameter greater than 6 mm
  • Recent changes (size, shape, colour)
  • Deep structures (pigment network)

Key points

  • Local anaesthesia — painless procedure
  • Removal and consultation possible in the same visit
  • Systematic histopathological analysis
  • Results communicated within 10–15 business days
  • Optimised healing using plastic surgery techniques

Duration: 30–45 min (consultation + removal)

Reimbursement: Partially reimbursed if medical indication confirmed. Bring your ID card and health insurance card.

Suspicious Mole Removal

When a lesion is identified as suspicious or atypical, our plastic surgeon performs a surgical excision under local anaesthesia. The procedure involves removing the lesion with an appropriate safety margin, then suturing the skin with maximum aesthetic care: respect for natural tension lines, ultra-fine superficial sutures, repositioning the scar within natural skin folds where possible.

The lesion is systematically sent for histopathological analysis at a specialist laboratory. Results are communicated to you within 10 to 15 business days.

Comfort & Aesthetics

Cosmetic Mole Removal

Would you like to have a benign mole removed for aesthetic or comfort reasons — because it bothers you visually, catches on clothing or jewellery, or causes irritation? Our plastic surgeons perform this type of removal with the same surgical rigour as for a medical indication.

The aesthetic result is their priority: every incision is planned to minimise the visible scar in the long term. A benign mole is confirmed during the prior consultation. Removal can in most cases be performed on the same day. The lesion is nonetheless sent for histopathological analysis to rule out any residual risk.

  • Benign mole confirmed before removal
  • Optimal scarring result — plastic surgery technique
  • Removal possible on the day of consultation
  • Histopathological analysis included

Duration: 20–40 min depending on number and location

Reimbursement: Not reimbursed (cosmetic procedure). Some supplementary insurers provide partial coverage — check with your insurer.

Why choose a plastic surgeon?

A plastic surgeon uses specialised suture techniques that minimise long-term scarring — something most GPs or dermatologists cannot guarantee.

  • Incision placed along natural tension lines
  • Ultra-fine superficial sutures (absorbable or not)
  • Scar repositioned within natural skin folds
  • Personalised post-operative follow-up
