Our approach

What only a plastic surgeon can do

Unlike conventional dermatology, our plastic surgeons combine two skills that most practitioners exercise separately: the precise analysis of skin lesions and their surgical excision. This means a single consultation is sufficient in most cases — less waiting, fewer appointments, and optimised healing through plastic surgery techniques (respecting skin tension lines, ultra-fine superficial sutures, repositioning scars within natural creases).

Prevention

Full Skin Check

Complete examination of all your skin lesions using the Heine DeltaOne dermatoscope. Your surgeon analyses every mole according to the ABCDE rule and identifies those requiring monitoring or excision.

Duration: 20–30 min

Suspect Mole Excision

Surgical excision under local anaesthesia of an atypical or suspect mole, followed by laboratory pathological analysis. In the majority of cases, excision is performed during the same consultation as the diagnosis.

Duration: 30–45 min

Cosmetic Mole Removal

Diagnostic technology

Heine DeltaOne — The world reference in dermoscopy

The Heine DeltaOne dermatoscope is the reference instrument used by the world's most demanding specialists. Its high-resolution optics and polarised LED lighting allow deep skin structures — invisible to the naked eye — to be visualised clearly. This precision equipment enables our surgeons to make accurate diagnoses and avoid unnecessary excisions.

How it works

3 simple steps

  • 1

    Book your appointment

    Reserve your consultation online in a few clicks. No referral or prescription required.

  • 2

    Consultation & Diagnosis

    Your surgeon examines your lesions with the Heine DeltaOne dermatoscope and provides a precise diagnosis, clearly explaining whether excision is recommended.

  • 3

    Excision if indicated

    In most cases, excision can be performed the same day. The lesion is sent for pathological analysis. Results within 10 to 15 working days.

Insurance Reimbursement

Surgical excision of a suspect mole is partially reimbursed by Belgian mutual insurance when medically indicated. Cosmetic excision is not reimbursed.

Please bring your identity card and your insurance card to your appointment.

Quick answers

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a referral or prescription?
No. You can book directly online without a doctor's referral or prescription.
Is the excision reimbursed by insurance?
It is partially reimbursed by Belgian mutual insurance if there is a medical indication. Cosmetic excision is not covered by standard insurance.
How long does the consultation take?
Allow 20 to 45 minutes depending on the case — a simple skin check or a consultation with same-day excision.
