Diagnostic technology

Heine DeltaOne — Our Diagnostic Tool

Dermoscopy is at the heart of our practice. Without this precision instrument, many atypical lesions would go undetected during a standard visual examination.

Technical features

Advanced capabilities

  • High-resolution optics Visualisation of deep melanocytic structures, invisible to the naked eye.
  • Polarised LED lighting Elimination of skin reflections, enabling in-depth analysis without skin contact.
  • Clinical ergonomics Optimal comfort for the patient, maximum precision for the surgeon.
  • Digital documentation available Traceability, longitudinal monitoring and comparison of lesions over time.

Clinical benefits

Why dermoscopy changes everything

Digital dermoscopy detects signs of atypicality invisible to the naked eye — irregular pigment networks, atypical globules, blue-white veils — which may indicate early malignant transformation.

Used by our surgeons, the Heine DeltaOne significantly reduces unnecessary removals and improves diagnostic precision. The result: fewer superfluous procedures, and better detection of high-risk lesions.

What the naked eye cannot see

  • Irregular pigment networks
  • Atypical peripheral globules
  • Blue-white veil (melanoma signal)
  • Regression structures
  • Atypical vascularisation
